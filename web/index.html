🌍 Introduction

English has become a global language that plays an important role in education, business, and international communication. In today’s modern world, the ability to communicate effectively in English is not only an advantage but also a necessity.

This website provides a digital learning environment where students can access course materials, understand key concepts, and practice their speaking skills in a more interactive and engaging way.

🎯 Learning Objectives

The main objectives of this learning platform are:

  • To improve students’ speaking and communication skills
  • To help students understand real-life English usage
  • To develop confidence in expressing ideas in English
  • To provide practical learning materials from classroom presentations

📖 Course Scope

The English Conversation 2 course focuses on practical communication skills used in various real-life contexts. Students will learn how to:

  • Deliver storytelling effectively
  • Present news using proper structure
  • Create persuasive advertisements
  • Communicate in business situations (buying & selling)
  • Understand the importance of English in professional environments

💡 Benefits of Learning Through This Website

By using this website, students will gain several benefits:

  • Easy access to all learning materials in one place
  • Better understanding through structured explanations
  • Opportunities to practice English conversation independently
  • Improved confidence in speaking and presenting ideas

💡 Learning Strategies & Tips

This section provides comprehensive strategies to improve English communication skills through structured learning approaches. Each skill is explained with practical methods that can be applied in daily learning activities.

🎧 Listening Strategy

Listening is a fundamental skill in language acquisition because it builds understanding of pronunciation, intonation, and real-life communication patterns used by native speakers.

📌 Learning Focus

Students should not focus on understanding every single word, but rather the main idea and key expressions used in conversations.

📌 Step-by-Step Strategy

  • Step 1: Listen without subtitles to understand general meaning
  • Step 2: Listen again using subtitles
  • Step 3: Write down unfamiliar words
  • Step 4: Repeat and imitate pronunciation (shadowing)

🎵 Recommended Songs (Clear Pronunciation)

  • Perfect – Ed Sheeran
  • Someone Like You – Adele
  • Count on Me – Bruno Mars

💡 Additional Tips

  • Use podcasts for beginners
  • Practice listening at least 15 minutes daily
  • Focus on accent and stress patterns

🗣️ Speaking Strategy

Speaking is the productive skill that reflects how well a learner can use English in real communication. Confidence plays a major role in developing speaking ability.

📌 Learning Focus

The goal of speaking is not grammatical perfection, but the ability to express ideas clearly.

📌 Practice Techniques

  • Practice self-introduction daily
  • Use mirror speaking to build confidence
  • Record your voice and evaluate pronunciation
  • Practice role play based on real-life situations

💡 Example Practice

Introduce yourself for 1 minute using simple sentences:
"My name is..., I study..., I like..., My goal is..."

💡 Tips

  • Do not be afraid of making mistakes
  • Focus on fluency first, then accuracy
  • Speak slowly and clearly

📖 Reading Strategy

Reading helps students improve vocabulary, grammar understanding, and comprehension skills

📌 Learning Focus

Students should develop the ability to understand texts efficiently using different reading techniques.

📚 Reading Techniques

  • Skimming: reading quickly to get the main idea
  • Scanning: searching for specific information
  • Intensive Reading: analyzing detailed information

📌 Practice Method

  • Read short articles or news daily
  • Highlight unfamiliar vocabulary
  • Guess meaning from context
  • Read aloud to improve pronunciation

💡 Tips

  • Do not translate word by word
  • Focus on sentence meaning
  • Build vocabulary gradually

✍️ Writing Strategy

Writing is a productive skill that helps students organize ideas and apply grammar rules correctly.

📌 Learning Focus

Students should focus on clarity, structure, and coherence in writing.

📌 Writing Structure

  • Subject + Verb + Object (basic sentence)
  • Use simple present tense for daily writing
  • Use connectors (and, but, because)

📌 Practice Method

  • Write daily journals (5–10 sentences)
  • Describe your daily activities
  • Rewrite sentences to improve structure

💡 Tips

  • Start with simple sentences
  • Avoid complex grammar at the beginning
  • Check grammar using tools

🚀 General Learning Strategy

Effective English learning requires consistency, practice, and the integration of all skills.

  • Practice English every day
  • Combine listening, speaking, reading, and writing
  • Use English in daily conversations
  • Think in English instead of translating
  • Stay motivated and set learning goals
